Config PayPal

To active PayPal payment go to developer page https://developer.paypal.com/home and sign in with your paypal credentials

- After you sign in to your account, select your app environment (Sandbox or Live) and click "Create"

- Now set a name for your app and select "Merchant" as in this screenshot then click "Create App" button

- Now you can see your app keys, go to LION STORE dashboard
https://your-domain/dashboard/services/paypal and set them
