Twilio SMS Service

LION STORE uses Twilio as default SMS service to send your customers sms messages, but If you want another one, you can contact me on WhatsApp (Number in Introduction page) and I'll be glad to help you.

- Login to your Twilio account https://www.twilio.com/login

- Click Twilio logo "View All Accounts"

- Then click Create New Account button

- Give your account a name and click Verify

- Verify your E-mail address and your phone number

- Now use same settings and click Get started with Twilio button

- Then you can see your Twilio keys, copy them and go to your LION STORE dashboard
https://your-domain.com/dashboard/services/twilio and paste them and don't forget to get a number also
