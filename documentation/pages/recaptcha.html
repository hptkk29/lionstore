Google reCaptcha

reCAPTCHA is a CAPTCHA system that enables web hosts to distinguish between human and automated access to websites. The original version asked users to decipher hard to read text or match images

- First sign in to your Google account and then go to this page https://www.google.com/recaptcha/admin

- Click the "Plus" icon like the screenshot

- Now set a name and choose recaptcha version and put your domain name then check terms checkbox and click submit

- Finally you can see your public and secret keys, copy them to your dashboard => Security Settings
https://your-domain.com/dashboard/settings/security
